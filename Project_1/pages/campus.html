Sports calibrate a student’s mind, body and soul. Sipna focuses on a balanced blend of sports and studies which makes the Sipnoids active and energetic. Students of our college participate in various sport activities organized at various collegiate and inter collegiate levels. We have student teams for various sports like foot ball, cricket, basket ball, volley ball, kabaddi etc. Many students have also represented at the University, National & International level competitions of various sports & are honored as the color coat holders. The college provides various facilities to the students such as well equipped Table Tennis Hall , Badminton hall , Cricket play ground , Basket ball court.

The college has its own Gymnasium equipped with all the modern fitness instruments. All the students make use of these facilities to maintain physical and mental fitness.
